About Us
Welcome to Gracepath

Best Medical & Healthcare Needs to Our Patients

Grace Path Diagnostic Centre (GPDC) is a first-class medical diagnostic centre located in a spacious purpose built 3 Story building on No. 66, Igi Olugbin road, Pedro Road Ladi-Lak Bariga, Lagos.
GPDC provides wide scope for detection of ailments and facilities for a detailed medical check-up through diagnostic procedures for Patients, Practicing Doctors, Clinics, HMOs, and Corporate Organizations.

Our vision is to meet the diagnostic needs of all our client, as well as serve local hospital within our geographic location and beyond; hereby, meeting and surpassing the expectations of our clients with not only accurate, fast, seamless diagnostics, and also honouring confidentiality.

Our mission is to offer medical diagnostic services with superlative measures of Efficiency, Ethics and Quality at a reasonable and affordable price to Patients, Practicing Doctors, Clinics, HMOs, and Corporate Organizations.

We provide accurate, reliable, and timely medical results in the following areas:
